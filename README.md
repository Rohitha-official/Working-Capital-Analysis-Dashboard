# Working-Capital-Analysis-Dashboard
## Project Overview

This Tableau project analyzes Working Capital performance through three business-focused dashboards:

1. Accounts Receivable Performance Dashboard
2. Accounts Payable Performance Dashboard
3. Credit & Risk Dashboard

The project helps finance teams monitor collections, supplier payments, customer credit exposure, and working capital efficiency.

---

## Dashboard 1: Accounts Receivable Performance

### KPIs

- Total Customers
- Total Receivables
- Total Invoices
- Average Credit Limit

### Visualizations

- Outstanding Receivables by Region
- Outstanding Receivables by Aging Bucket
- Top 10 Customers by Outstanding Receivables
- Top 10 Highest Outstanding Invoices

---

## Dashboard 2: Accounts Payable Performance

### KPIs

- Total Suppliers
- Total Payables
- Total Invoices
- Total Late Payment Fees

### Visualizations

- Supplier Distribution by Vendor Type
- Outstanding Amount by Spend Category
- Paid vs Unpaid Invoices
- Top 10 Suppliers by Invoice Amount
- Top 10 Suppliers by Late Payment Fees

---

## Dashboard 3: Credit & Risk Dashboard

### KPIs

- Total Outstanding Receivables
- Total Credit Exposure
- Open Invoices
- Total Customers

### Visualizations

- Top 10 Customers by Credit Limit
- Outstanding Amount by Dunn Level
- Top 10 Customers by Outstanding Receivables
- Open vs Closed Receivables

## Business Recommendations

- Focus collection efforts on 90+ day receivables.
- Monitor customers with high credit exposure.
- Reduce supplier late-payment fees.
- Improve cash flow through better receivable management.
- Strengthen credit risk monitoring processes.
